Our Journey

Milestones of Outpost Scaling

From our first clinic promise to 140+ active water, learning, and nutrition grids globally.

2012 — The Promise
Founded by Dr. Anita Sharma in Kochi to coordinate emergency pediatric surgery funds for families waiting on critical clinical slots. First 12 surgeries funded in month one.
2016 — The First Water Station
Designed and engineered solar-powered reverse osmosis filtration plants in rural Jodhpur villages suffering arsenic water contamination. 800 families immediately supplied.
2020 — Kitchen Hub Scaling
Launched ZeroHunger community kitchens to cook and distribute hot vegetable meal boxes during local supply disruptions. 14 kitchens now serving 1,200+ meals daily.
2024 — Starlink Forest Classrooms
Equipped remote Mayurbhanj jungle schools with photovoltaic battery cells and Starlink terminals for daily tablet curriculum modules. 3,200 students now online.
