Our Impact Journey

Program Milestones

Key moments that shaped our humanitarian programs and expanded our reach.

2012 — First Mobile Clinic
Launched our very first mobile medical clinic in Salem, Tamil Nadu, serving 5,000+ patients in the first year.
2015 — Digital Schools Launch
Introduced our first solar-powered digital classrooms in Odisha, bringing tablet-based learning to 3,000+ students.
2018 — First Solar RO Plant
Installed our first solar-powered reverse-osmosis water plant in Rajasthan, providing clean water to 10,000+ people daily.
2026 — 1 Million Meals Served
Celebrated the milestone of serving 1 million nutritious meals through our Zero Hunger Kitchen Hubs.
